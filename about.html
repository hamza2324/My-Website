Our Story

Founded in 2019, AI Automation Agency began with a simple observation: businesses were drowning in repetitive tasks while powerful automation tools remained underutilized due to complexity and lack of expertise.

We started as a small team of automation specialists passionate about n8n's workflow capabilities. As AI evolved, we expanded our expertise to include GenAI integration, creating comprehensive automation solutions that combine workflow automation with artificial intelligence.

Today, we've helped over 50 businesses transform their operations, saving thousands of hours annually and enabling teams to focus on strategic work that drives real business value.
