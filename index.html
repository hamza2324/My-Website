FAQ: GEO and AI Search Optimization Steps

What is the first GEO quick win for low-impression websites?

Rewrite your first paragraph so it gives a direct answer in 2-3 lines, then support it with structured FAQs and internal links.

Should every page include FAQ schema?

Only include FAQ schema when the questions are visible on the page and directly relevant to the page topic.

How many GEO-optimized posts should I publish monthly?

Start with at least two high-quality posts per month that target conversational long-tail queries and include original insights.

Can GEO help with leads, not just impressions?

Yes. Combine AI-search-friendly content with clear service CTAs, contact forms, and trust signals to convert visibility into leads.

What does an AI SEO checklist include in 2026?

Direct answers, schema coverage, entity consistency, topical clusters, citation-worthy content, and monthly AI visibility tracking.
