Frequently Asked Questions

What is an FYP project?

FYP stands for Final Year Project. It is a capstone project that demonstrates real-world skills and problem solving.

What are the best project ideas?

The best ideas are realistic in scope, aligned with your interests, and solve a meaningful problem in your domain.

What are the 4 types of projects?

Most FYPs fall into software applications, research or analysis projects, systems implementation, or hardware/IoT builds.

Which topic is best for a project?

Pick a topic that matches your skill level, has clear scope, and can be completed in one or two semesters.
