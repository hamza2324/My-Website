FAQ

AI SEO Checklist FAQ

What is a GEO-optimized blog post?

A post designed for both humans and AI systems using clear answers, structured headings, schema, and trustworthy author signals.

How long should AI-optimized articles be?

Length depends on intent, but depth and clarity matter more than word count. Cover the topic fully with useful, scannable structure.

Do I need keywords in every heading?

No. Use primary keywords naturally and prioritize semantic relevance over repetition.

Can older posts be improved for GEO?

Yes. Add direct answer summaries, FAQ sections, updated stats, and stronger links to commercial pages.

What should I measure monthly?

Search impressions, clicks, AI-brand mentions, assisted conversions, and top pages that attract qualified traffic.
